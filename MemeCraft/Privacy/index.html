Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 27 Oct 2023

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information we receive from users of the App.

Information Collection and Use

As an image editing app with some features available to 'pro' users we may collect necessary device and Account information to accurately provide 'pro' services to subscribed users.

The images created by users are stored in app itself, some images are stored temporarily while some, as chosen by user are saved on disk.

Deletion of data

Temporary images are deleted periodically. Images stored on disk are available for users to delete from options on homepage. Uninstallation of app will ensure deletion of all locally stored data. Data related to purchases are needed for serving active subscriptions and cannot be deleted.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may make changes to this Privacy Policy, in which case we will notify you before such changes take effect by posting the updated policy through the Services or otherwise in accordance with applicable law. Any changes to this Privacy Policy will take effect after a reasonable time period as specified in the notice of such changes.
